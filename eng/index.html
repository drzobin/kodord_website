DDMail

DDMail is an email system designed with security as its primary focus. It has been developed to withstand threats from advanced adversarial actors. DDMail follows the design principles: "Less code, less attack surface", "Least privilege", "Default deny" and "Assume breach". DDMail employs various forms of separation and encryption to protect users' data. It is available as a SaaS solution or can be run On-Prem.

DDMail SaaS

Kodord AB provides DDMail as a SaaS service on the internet, hosted on servers located in Sweden. You can learn more about the service at www.ddmail.se.

DDMail On-Prem

Kodord AB offers support, training, installation assistance, and consulting services for On-Prem installations of DDMail. Contact us for details.
